# [Home Screen + Top Nav] - Contentstack Home and Top Navigation

This page explains how the Contentstack Home screen and the top navigation bar are organized, who they are designed for, and when to use them to quickly access products, modules, learning resources, and key workspace actions.

### Item 1

#### Article section

##### Heading

Contentstack Home and Top Navigation

##### Content

The home screen and the top navigation menu are designed to enhance user experience by providing a consolidated view of your workspace while allowing easy access to the tools and information you need the most.

Let’s take a closer look.

## Contentstack Home - Your Central Dashboard
The home screen serves as the starting point of your workflow in Contentstack. It provides a centralized dashboard view of all the products within Contentstack, including learning resources, platform updates, and quick links.

The home screen is organized into multiple sections to give you a quick, intuitive view of your workspace.
- **Explore Apps**: Navigate to Contentstack’s product suite from this section.**Note:** By default, all apps are displayed on the home screen to provide complete visibility into the Contentstack ecosystem. However, access to each app may vary based on your user role and your organization’s subscription plan.
- **Learning Modules**: Stay sharp with curated training courses from Contentstack Academy, including:Understanding Contentstack
- Launch Foundations
- Headless CMS courses
- Certification paths for various roles
- **Quick Links**: Located on the right, this section offers direct access to:Academy
- Developer Docs
- Contentstack Community on Discord
- **Upcoming Events**: Catch up on:Events (eg., ContentCon)
- Industry articles and announcements
- Featured insights
- **Changelog**: Get a quick overview of platform updates, feature releases, and SDK improvements. Click through to see the complete list in a new tab.
- **Contentstack Pulse**: Discover stories, podcasts, and insights from leaders who are using digital technology to drive change across industries.

**Tip:** Return to the home screen anytime by clicking the Contentstack logo or selecting **Home** from the 9-dot grid dropdown.

## Top Navigation Bar
The top navigation bar ensures essential tools and actions are always accessible. It streamlines browsing across stacks, products, and workspace areas.

The top navigation is designed to provide consistent access to Contentstack’s core modules.
- **Menu Item Navigation**: Key CMS modules such as Dashboard, Entries, Assets, Content Models, and Releases—along with similar modules for other Contentstack apps—are available as direct menu items in the top navigation bar.
- **Product Menu (9-dot Grid)**: Click the grid icon on the top-right corner to access all Contentstack products. You can also find quick link to **Favorites**.
- **Profile Settings**: Easily switch between organizations and update profile settings such as your profile image, password, two-factor authentication, or others.

Contentstack Home acts as your central dashboard and the top navigation allows seamless traversing across modules, enabling you to navigate Contentstack efficiently and stay focused on building powerful digital experiences.

## Common questions

**How do I return to the Contentstack Home screen?**  
Return to the home screen anytime by clicking the Contentstack logo or selecting **Home** from the 9-dot grid dropdown.

**Where do I find all Contentstack products?**  
Click the **Product Menu (9-dot Grid)** on the top-right corner to access all Contentstack products.

**Why can’t I access some apps shown on the Home screen?**  
Access to each app may vary based on your user role and your organization’s subscription plan.

**What can I do from Profile Settings in the top navigation?**  
You can switch between organizations and update profile settings such as your profile image, password, two-factor authentication, or others.
