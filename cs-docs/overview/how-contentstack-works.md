# [Introduction to Contentstack - a Headless CMS] - How Contentstack Works

This page explains how Contentstack works at a high level, outlining the core workflow for managing content. It is intended for anyone new to Contentstack (including content authors, admins, and developers) who needs to understand the basic steps used to structure, create, and publish content across channels.

## How Contentstack Works

Managing content in Contentstack is easy and does not involve any coding. It’s a simple 3-step process.

## Model your content

Define the structure of your content in a platform-independent way. This involves creating a content type and adding relevant fields such as “single-line textbox,” “date,” “file”. Adding fields is a simple drag-and-drop process.

## Create content

Once the required content types are ready, any user can add content, which is like filling up a form. Multiple users can work together in teams to create important content in multiple languages.

## Publish content

The content, once created, can be published to your website, mobile app, or any other channel. The same content can be reused for any platform.

## Common questions

### Do I need coding knowledge to manage content in Contentstack?

No. “Managing content in Contentstack is easy and does not involve any coding.”

### What are the three steps in the Contentstack content workflow?

Model your content, Create content, and Publish content.

### Can multiple users collaborate and create content in multiple languages?

Yes. “Multiple users can work together in teams to create important content in multiple languages.”

### Where can I publish the content created in Contentstack?

You can publish content “to your website, mobile app, or any other channel.”
