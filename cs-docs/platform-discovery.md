# [Platform Discovery] - Platform Discovery

This page explains what Platform Discovery is in Contentstack, how to access it, and how to use feature usage statuses and Impact Areas filtering to identify and adopt capabilities that align with your organization’s goals.

## Platform Discovery

Platform Discovery is your central dashboard for exploring Contentstack’s capabilities. It provides a unified view of all the features on offer, highlighting active features and uncovering powerful tools you may not yet be utilizing.

Use Platform Discovery to:
- **Maximize ROI:** Identify enabled features that are currently sitting idle.
- **Align with business goals:** Find specific capabilities that solve for speed, efficiency, or personalization.
- **Scale your stack:** Discover advanced features to upgrade your digital experiences.

Accessing Platform Discovery:
- Open the “App Switcher” in the top navigation bar.
- Select **Platform Discovery** from the menu.

## Understanding Feature Usage

The dashboard displays feature cards labeled by their current usage status within your organization:
- **In-Use:** The feature is active and in at least one stack or project.
- **Not in Use:** You have access to this feature, but it is not currently utilized. This is an opportunity to drive immediate value.
- **Requires Upgrade:** The feature is currently available in your plan. Contact support or your CSM to unlock it.

**Tip:** Hover over any card and click **Learn More** to access documentation or watch a quick Academy video.

## Filtering by Impact Areas

Focus on what matters most to your current strategy. Use the **Impact Areas** filter to view features aligned with specific business outcomes, such as:
- **Efficiency:** Automate operations to save team hours.
- **Productivity:** Streamline editorial and developer workflows.
- **Time-to-market:** Accelerate launch cycles across channels.
- **Personalization:** Tailor content for specific audience segments.
- **Content ROI:** Leverage analytics to drive performance.
- **Workflows & Collaboration:** Unblock teams and streamline approvals.
- **Performance & Scalability:** Ensure enterprise-grade stability and growth.

To filter your view:
- Click the **Impact Areas** dropdown in the top-right corner of your dashboard.
- Select your desired outcome (e.g., **Time-to-market**) to see relevant tools.

Use Platform Discovery regularly to assess and optimize your Contentstack usage. For features marked **Requires Upgrade**, reach out to our [support](mailto:suppoer@contentstack.com) team to enable them for your organization.
