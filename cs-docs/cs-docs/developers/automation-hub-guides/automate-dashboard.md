# Automate Dashboard

This page explains what the Automate Dashboard is in Contentstack Automate, who it’s for (teams building or monitoring Agents and Automations), and when to use it (to visualize automation health, track live activity, and review execution history while optimizing workflows).

## Automate Dashboard

The **Automate Dashboard** is your central workspace to build, manage, and monitor everything you automate within Contentstack. It brings together Agents, Automations, and Executions tracking into one powerful overview, helping you optimize workflows and scale confidently.

Whether you are creating manual workflows or intelligent Agents, the Dashboard provides a unified place to control and optimize your automation strategies.

## Why use the Automate Dashboard?

The Automate Dashboard is designed to help you:

- **Visualize your automation landscape: **Quickly see how many Agents and Automations you have and how they are performing.
- **Monitor success rates and execution times: **Understand which workflows are running smoothly and where you might need adjustments.
- **Track real-time activity: **Stay updated on every action your Agents take, using the live feed.
- **Scale your operations: **Easily build new automations or Agents as your needs grow, without losing control or visibility.

By centralizing this information, Automate makes it easy for teams to move fast while staying aligned on automation goals.

## Key Features

Here are the key features of the Automate Dashboard to explore:

### Overview

At the top of the dashboard, you get a quick glance at your automation health:

- Total count of **Agents** and **Automations** created in a project.
- **Success Rate **reflects the total count of successful execution for Agents and Automations. Suppose, there are a total of 100 executions in a day, out of which 60 are successful and 40 are in some other statuses such as Failed, Running, Paused, etc. So the success rate would be **60% **for the day.**Note: **The data is recorded for **24 hours**.
- **Execution Summary (Today) **to track the total number of executions in a day for both Agents and Automations.
- **Average Execution Time** is the time taken by each Automation and Agent (in a project) to run.

### Live Feed

The Live Feed brings transparency into how your Agents are currently working. You can view:

- How many active Agents are running.
- View step-by-step breakdowns of tasks.
- Understand the AI reasoning behind the Agent’s decisions.

This visibility is especially useful for complex workflows where dynamic responses or multiple systems are involved.

### Agents and Automations

From the Dashboard, you can quickly check all the active Automations and Agents.

- **Agents: **View all active Agents, along with their Abilities, Status, and Models directly from the Agent cards.
- **Automations: **View all active Automations, including the number of steps, creator name, etc., from the Automation cards.

You can monitor each Agent or Automation individually, track their usage, and refine them based on performance insights.

### Execution Log

The Execution Log view gives you full transparency into every workflow run. You can filter executions, monitor success and failure rates, and troubleshoot based on detailed logs — helping you optimize and scale confidently.

**Note:** You can filter execution history for up to **90 days at a time**.

## How to Get Started

- Log in to your [Contentstack account](https://www.contentstack.com/login) and click the **Automate** icon in the left navigation panel.
- Select your project and then the automation.
- Navigate to the **Dashboard** in the left navigation panel.
- Use the **Overview** section to monitor the health of your workflows.
- Track Agent activity in real time through the **Live** **Feed**.
- Dive into the **Execution Log** section to review daily executions for both Agents and Automations.

## Best Practices

- **Start small: **Build a few workflows first, then expand as your needs grow.
- **Monitor regularly: **Use metrics like Success Rate and Average Execution Time to fine-tune workflows.
- **Use Agents for dynamic tasks: **For workflows that require AI reasoning, start using Agents early.
- **Centralize logic: **Store custom scripts and reusable workflows in Automate to stay organized.
- **Stay agile: **Review the Execution Log frequently to adjust strategies based on real data.

The** Automate Dashboard** is more than a monitoring tool, it is the **control** **center** for your automation strategy. It empowers you to create smarter workflows, monitor them at scale, and continuously improve based on real-time insights. Whether you are building manual Automations or leveraging Agents, the Dashboard makes sure you always have full control over your automation journey.

## Common questions

### Where do I find the Automate Dashboard?
Log in to your Contentstack account, click the **Automate** icon in the left navigation panel, select your project and automation, then navigate to the **Dashboard** in the left navigation panel.

### What time range does the Success Rate cover?
**Note: **The data is recorded for **24 hours**.

### How far back can I filter the Execution Log?
**Note:** You can filter execution history for up to **90 days at a time**.

### What’s the difference between Live Feed and Execution Log?
The **Live Feed** shows real-time Agent activity and step-by-step task breakdowns, while the **Execution Log** provides detailed logs for workflow runs to monitor outcomes and troubleshoot.
