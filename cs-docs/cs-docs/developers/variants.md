### Listing page

#### Heading

Variants

#### Introduction

Variants, currently in the Early Access Phase, enables you to create distinct content experiences tailored to specific audiences, campaigns, or contexts. By defining multiple variants for an entry, you can customize content, visuals, and offers based on user characteristics such as location, preferences, or behavior. This approach enhances personalization, ensuring that each user receives relevant and engaging content that resonates with their unique needs.

Variants streamline the process of delivering targeted experiences, helping you effectively connect with diverse audience segments.
