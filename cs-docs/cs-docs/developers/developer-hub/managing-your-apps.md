# [Developer Hub guides] - Managing your Apps

This page explains how to manage all your installed/authorized apps and installation requests from the **Manage** section in the Marketplace. It is intended for developers and administrators who need to view, configure, or uninstall apps in their Contentstack stack.

## Managing your Apps

You can manage all your installed/authorized apps and installation requests from the **Manage** section (tab next to **Discover** in the left navigation panel).

To see the installed/authorized apps in your stack, follow the steps below:
- Log in to your [Contentstack Account](https://app.contentstack.com/#!/login).
- In the left navigation panel, click the** Marketplace** icon.
- On the screen that appears, click the **Manage** button.
- Under the **Manage **> **Installed Apps** section, you will find your installed apps. Here you can configure or uninstall your apps as needed.
- Under the **Manage **> **Authorized Apps** section, you will find your authorized apps.

## Common questions

**Q: Where do I find the Manage section for apps?**  
A: In the Marketplace, open the **Manage** tab (next to **Discover** in the left navigation panel).

**Q: What is the difference between Installed Apps and Authorized Apps?**  
A: **Installed Apps** are apps installed in your stack that you can configure or uninstall, while **Authorized Apps** are apps that have been authorized.

**Q: Do I need to log in before managing apps?**  
A: Yes, you must log in to your Contentstack account before accessing the Marketplace and the **Manage** section.
