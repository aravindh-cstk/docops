# [Marketplace guides and apps] - About Marketplace

This page explains what Contentstack Marketplace is and how to access it from within the Contentstack app. It is intended for developers and users who want to discover, browse, and install apps and resources to extend Contentstack, and should be used when you are getting started with Marketplace navigation and access.

## About Marketplace

Contentstack Marketplace is a hub for apps and other resources that help you extend the capabilities of our core CMS and customize its functionalities. It is a one-stop platform to discover, browse, and install tools to enhance the Contentstack experience by connecting to various third-party services in a simple one-click installation. The apps and resources on Marketplace are created by Contentstack and our partners.

To access Marketplace, log in to your [Contentstack account](https://app.contentstack.com/#!/login).

On the left-hand side primary navigation, you will find a new icon for Marketplace (as shown below). Click on the icon to go to the Marketplace.

## Common questions

### Who can use Contentstack Marketplace?
Anyone who can log in to a Contentstack account can access Marketplace.

### What is Contentstack Marketplace used for?
It is used to discover, browse, and install apps and resources that extend the capabilities of the core CMS and connect to third-party services.

### Where do I find Marketplace in the Contentstack UI?
On the left-hand side primary navigation, there is an icon for Marketplace; click it to go to Marketplace.

### Who creates the apps and resources available on Marketplace?
The apps and resources on Marketplace are created by Contentstack and our partners.
