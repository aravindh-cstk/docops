# [Second level navigation] - Working with Content Models

This page introduces Contentstack Marketplace Content Models, explaining what they are and how developers can use them to import, view, and edit content type schemas within a stack. Read this when setting up or working with structured content types and prebuilt models in Contentstack.

### Item 1

#### Listing page

##### Heading

Working with Content Models

##### Introduction

The Contentstack Content Models are a blueprint or schema of a content type that define the structure, fields, and relationships of the content. With the Contentstack Marketplace Content Models, you can import, view and edit their schema, and use them within your stack.

## Common questions

### What are Contentstack Content Models used for?
They are a blueprint or schema of a content type that define the structure, fields, and relationships of the content.

### What can I do with Contentstack Marketplace Content Models?
You can import, view and edit their schema, and use them within your stack.

### Where can I find prebuilt Content Models?
They are listed under the “Prebuilt Content Models” section on this page, with links to individual models such as Website Homepage, Website Header, and Blog Landing Page.
