##### Content

The Visual Builder in Contentstack offers a powerful, intuitive interface that allows you to build and manage web pages directly on a visual canvas. This tool empowers content creators and marketers to edit content on a live preview of the site, streamlining the content update process. You can seamlessly modify web pages, rearrange components, and create new content without technical expertise.

Experience the power of a true WYSIWYG (What You See Is What You Get) interface, bridging the gap between content creation and its final presentation. Visual Builder empowers seamless collaboration between marketers and developers, fostering a more efficient and intuitive content management process.

## Key Features and Benefits
- **Customizable Interface**: Easily reconstruct pages by moving or adding components and content blocks.
- **Real-Time Preview**: Visualize changes and edits as you make them, ensuring the final output aligns with your vision.
- **Content Personalization**: Tailor content to specific audiences or contexts to enhance user engagement.
- **Workflow Management**: Streamline content creation and publishing processes with customizable workflows.
- **Multi-Channel Publishing**: Effortlessly publish content across various digital channels, including websites, mobile apps, and more.

With Visual Builder, simplify content creation and management, empowering your team to deliver exceptional digital experiences efficiently.

## Common questions

### Who is Visual Builder intended for?
Visual Builder is intended for content creators and marketers to edit content on a live preview of the site, and it also supports collaboration between marketers and developers.

### What does WYSIWYG mean in this context?
WYSIWYG stands for “What You See Is What You Get,” referring to editing content on a visual canvas that reflects the final presentation.

### Can Visual Builder be used without technical expertise?
Yes, it enables you to modify web pages, rearrange components, and create new content without technical expertise.

### What types of channels can content be published to?
It supports multi-channel publishing across various digital channels, including websites and mobile apps.
