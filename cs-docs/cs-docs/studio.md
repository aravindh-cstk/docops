##### Introduction

Studio is a web experience builder that empowers developers, designers, and marketers to collaboratively create, edit, and launch visually rich digital experiences without relying heavily on code. It combines visual composition with governance and flexibility, helping teams deliver production-ready pages using reusable components and real-time data.

Studio bridges the gap between design and development, accelerating time to market while ensuring enterprise-grade performance, scalability, and governance, all powered by Contentstack’s composable architecture.

**Note:** Studio is currently part of an Early Access Program and may not be available to all users. Contact the Contentstack [support](mailto:support@contentstack.com) team; they will coordinate with the appropriate stakeholders to review eligibility.
