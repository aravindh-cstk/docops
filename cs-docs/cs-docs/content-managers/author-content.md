## Common questions

### What is this page used for?
This page is a listing page that helps you navigate to Author Content documentation for creating and managing entries and assets.

### Who should use these links?
Content managers who need guidance on entries, assets, comments, versions, bulk operations, backup/restore, and live preview should use these links.

### What do flags like NEW and EARLY ACCESS indicate?
They indicate the status of specific documentation items, such as NEW or EARLY ACCESS, as shown alongside certain links.

### Where can I find FAQs for entries, assets, comments, drafts, and live preview?
Use the listed FAQ links such as /faqs/#entries-faqs, /faqs/#assets-faqs, /faqs/#entry-comments-faqs, /docs/faqs#drafts-and-auto-save-faqs, and /faqs#live-preview-faqs.
