# [Timeline] - About Timeline

This page explains what Timeline is and how content managers can use it to preview scheduled content changes before they go live, helping validate upcoming updates during planning and review.

## About Timeline

Timeline is a powerful feature tailored for content managers, allowing you to visualize future changes to your website content effortlessly. This feature enables you to preview how your site will look once scheduled updates are live, offering a comprehensive view of upcoming content transformations without the need to schedule content beforehand.

Consider the scenario where you've just updated your website content for an upcoming sale. With Timeline, you can easily preview scheduled content editions that are set to go live at specific dates and times. This ensures everything looks perfect and aligns with your vision before going live, simplifying your content management process.

## Key Benefits

- **Visualize Future Updates**: Gain insights into how scheduled updates will affect your site's look and feel.
- **Ensure Accuracy**: Verify that all elements of your future content align perfectly with your expectations.
- **Streamline Planning**: Simplify your content planning process by previewing changes ahead of time.

By leveraging Timeline, content managers can ensure that their websites will look exactly as intended when updates go live, enhancing the overall user experience and maintaining consistency across all scheduled changes.

## Common questions

### Who should use Timeline?
Content managers who want to preview how scheduled updates will affect their website content before those changes go live.

### Do I need to schedule content before using Timeline?
No. Timeline offers a comprehensive view of upcoming content transformations without the need to schedule content beforehand.

### What is the main benefit of Timeline?
It allows you to preview how your site will look once scheduled updates are live, helping ensure everything looks perfect and aligns with your vision before going live.

### When is Timeline especially useful?
When preparing time-based updates (for example, content for an upcoming sale) and you want to verify scheduled content editions set to go live at specific dates and times.
