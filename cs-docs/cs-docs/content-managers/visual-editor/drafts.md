# [Visual Editor] - Drafts

This page explains how to use the Drafts panel in Contentstack’s Visual Editor to track, compare, and revert unsaved changes before publishing. It is intended for content managers and editors working in Visual Experience who need to review draft modifications and restore saved content when necessary.

## Drafts

The Drafts panel allows you to track and manage unsaved content changes before publishing. It helps you compare versions, highlight differences, and restore saved content when needed.

To access the Drafts panel, log in to your [Contentstack account](https://www.contentstack.com/login/), and perform the following steps:
- Navigate to your [stack](/docs/developers/set-up-stack/about-stack) and select **Visual Experience**.
- Click **Editor** in the bottom pill menu.
- In the right sidebar, click the **Drafts** icon to view the list of modified fields.
- Click “Compare” next to an entry. A side-by-side view opens, showing the saved version and the current draft. Draft changes appear in red for quick identification.**Tip:** Modified fields are outlined in red on the canvas to help you quickly identify changes.

To remove all unsaved updates and restore the last saved version of the entry, click the **Revert** icon.

The Drafts panel helps you maintain full control over your content before publishing.

## Common questions

### What does the Drafts panel show?
It shows a list of modified fields and helps you track and manage unsaved content changes before publishing.

### How do I compare a saved version with my current draft?
Click “Compare” next to an entry to open a side-by-side view showing the saved version and the current draft.

### How do I discard unsaved changes?
Click the **Revert** icon to remove all unsaved updates and restore the last saved version of the entry.
