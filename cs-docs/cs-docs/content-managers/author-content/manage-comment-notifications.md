# [Author Content] - Manage Comment Notifications

This page explains how comment notifications work when someone tags you in a comment, and how to view and use those notifications. It is intended for content managers and authors who participate in discussions and need to navigate to tagged comment threads.

## Manage Comment Notifications

When anyone tags you in a specific comment, you receive an email as well as an in-app notification. To view these notifications, click on the “Bell” icon on the left navigation panel:

You can click on any notification to directly jump to that discussion.

## Common questions

### Do I receive both email and in-app notifications when someone tags me?
Yes. When anyone tags you in a specific comment, you receive an email as well as an in-app notification.

### Where do I find my in-app comment notifications?
Click on the “Bell” icon on the left navigation panel.

### Can I open the discussion directly from a notification?
Yes. You can click on any notification to directly jump to that discussion.
