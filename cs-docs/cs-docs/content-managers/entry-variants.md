# [Second level navigation] - Entry Variants

This page introduces Contentstack’s Entry Variants and provides navigation links for content managers to learn how to create, edit, publish, delete, and manage versions of entry variants, including understanding publishing behavior and limitations.

## Listing page

### Heading

Entry Variants

### Introduction

Contentstack's Entry Variants, currently in the Early Access Phase, enable you to create tailored versions of a single content entry, allowing you to customize the user experience for different audiences, campaigns, or contexts. By leveraging entry variants, you can maintain a unified content structure while offering personalized content that resonates with specific user segments.

This feature enhances your ability to deliver relevant information, promotions, or experiences based on various factors such as location, preferences, or seasonal campaigns. Whether you’re running a marketing initiative or targeting specific demographics, entry variants provide the flexibility you need to engage your audience effectively.
