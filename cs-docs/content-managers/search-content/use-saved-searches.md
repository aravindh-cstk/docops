# [Search Content] - Use Saved Searches

This page explains how to find and manage saved searches in Search Content. It is intended for content managers who use Search Content regularly and need to quickly reuse or rename saved search queries.

## Use Saved Searches

Once you save a search, it can be found in the “Saved Search” section on the extreme right-hand side of the search bar.

You can also find saved searches on the auto-suggest drop-down of the search bar in the header.

To edit a search’s name, click the edit icon.

**Note:** Saved searches are user specific. You can save a maximum of **500** searches. This means that your saved searches are visible only to you. They are not available to any other users of your stack or organization.

## Common questions

**How do I find my saved searches?**  
You can find them in the “Saved Search” section on the extreme right-hand side of the search bar, and in the auto-suggest drop-down of the search bar in the header.

**How do I rename a saved search?**  
To edit a search’s name, click the edit icon.

**Are saved searches shared with other users?**  
No. Saved searches are user specific and are visible only to you.

**How many saved searches can I save?**  
You can save a maximum of **500** searches.
