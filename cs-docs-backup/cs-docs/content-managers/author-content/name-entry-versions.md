# [Author Content | Entry Versions] - Name Entry Versions

This page explains how to assign custom names to entry versions in Contentstack to help content managers organize and identify specific versions more easily. Use it when you need to rename versions in an entry’s version history for clearer content management workflows.

### Item 1

#### Article section

##### Heading

Name Entry Versions

##### Content

Assigning custom names to versions can help you better organize and identify specific versions of your entries for improved content management.

By default, Contentstack assigns a version number to each entry (e.g., "Version 1," "Version 2," etc.) . To make versions easier to identify, you can assign custom names, such as"Production Ready" or "Do Not Edit."

To name an entry version, log in to your [Contentstack account](https://www.contentstack.com/login), and perform the following steps:
- Go to your [stack](../../developers/set-up-stack/about-stack.md) and click the “Entries” icon.
- Navigate to the entry you want to modify.
- Click the version icon in the top-right corner of the entry editor, or use the “Versions” icon in the right sidebar to open the **Version History** panel.
- Locate the version you want to name. Hover over it and click the “Rename” icon.
- Enter a custom name and confirm your changes by pressing “Enter” on your keyboard.**Note**: Version names can include only **alphanumeric characters**, **spaces**, **hyphens**, or **underscores**. Special characters such as slashes or symbols are not supported.

**Note**: Custom version names have a maximum limit of **32 characters**.

## Common questions

**Q: What characters are supported in custom version names?**  
A: Version names can include only **alphanumeric characters**, **spaces**, **hyphens**, or **underscores**. Special characters such as slashes or symbols are not supported.

**Q: What is the maximum length for a custom version name?**  
A: Custom version names have a maximum limit of **32 characters**.

**Q: Where do I rename a version in an entry?**  
A: Open the **Version History** panel, locate the version, hover over it, and click the “Rename” icon.
