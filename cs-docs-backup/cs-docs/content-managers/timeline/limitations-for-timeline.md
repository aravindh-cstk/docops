# [Timeline] - Limitations for Timeline

This page lists the limitations and compatibility requirements of the Timeline feature. It is intended for content managers and developers who need to understand when Timeline preview and scheduling will work (and when it will not) before enabling or using the feature in an organization.

## Limitations for Timeline

- The Timeline feature is only available if your organization has the Live Preview and Timeline plan enabled. Please contact the [support](mailto:support@contentstack.com) team to activate this plan for your organization.
- Timeline does not support websites generated using third-party builders or CMS platforms to produce HTML.
- The feature is not compatible with native mobile applications.
- You cannot preview entry content without the necessary permissions for accessing entries, specific environments, or published languages.
- Your website should support Iframes to prevent CORS errors during preview.
- You can preview only **one** temporary schedule at a time.
- You can preview a maximum of **10** dates simultaneously on the timeline.

## Common questions

### How do I enable the Timeline feature for my organization?
The Timeline feature is only available if your organization has the Live Preview and Timeline plan enabled. Please contact the [support](mailto:support@contentstack.com) team to activate this plan for your organization.

### Can I use Timeline with a site built using a third-party website builder?
Timeline does not support websites generated using third-party builders or CMS platforms to produce HTML.

### Why can’t I preview entry content in Timeline?
You cannot preview entry content without the necessary permissions for accessing entries, specific environments, or published languages.

### How many dates can I preview at once on the timeline?
You can preview a maximum of **10** dates simultaneously on the timeline.
